About Us

Specialising in a range of beautiful oak gifts - we aim to provide excellent quality & service. Oakleaf Commemorative Furniture Ltd is based in a traditional workshop in the heart of rural Somerset. An artisan woodworking & cabinet making business, producing bespoke pieces of furniture & a wide variety of woodworking projects since 2002. Each individual piece is handcrafted using traditional techniques & finished to reveal the natural splendour of solid oak.
